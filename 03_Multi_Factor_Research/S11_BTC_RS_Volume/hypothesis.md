# S11 – BTC Relative Strength + Volume Confirmation

## Motivation

BTC Relative Strength produced the strongest strategy developed during research, achieving a Sharpe ratio of 1.56.

Volume Leadership produced the highest Information Coefficient observed during exploratory analysis:

Volume IC = 0.048

However, the standalone volume strategy underperformed BTC Relative Strength.

This suggests volume may function better as a confirmation factor than as a standalone source of alpha.

---

## Research Question

Does volume contain information beyond BTC Relative Strength?

---

## Hypothesis

Assets that:

1. Outperform Bitcoin
2. Exhibit unusually strong trading volume

should outperform assets selected using BTC Relative Strength alone.

Volume may confirm institutional participation and capital inflows, increasing confidence in leadership signals.

---

## Expected Behaviour

Volume should improve asset selection quality by filtering weak relative-strength signals.

---

## Success Criteria

- Sharpe Ratio greater than S07 (1.561)
- Improved equity growth
- Similar or lower drawdown
- Demonstrate independent contribution from volume
