# S10 – BTC Relative Strength + Momentum

## Motivation

Previous research identified two highly successful factors:

1. Momentum
2. BTC Relative Strength

Momentum generated a Sharpe ratio of 1.285.

BTC Relative Strength generated a Sharpe ratio of 1.561.

Both factors appear to capture trend persistence, but it is unclear whether they represent the same underlying phenomenon.

---

## Research Question

Can BTC Relative Strength and Momentum be combined to produce stronger risk-adjusted returns than either factor individually?

---

## Hypothesis

Momentum captures absolute trend strength.

BTC Relative Strength captures leadership relative to the dominant market benchmark.

The combination should identify assets that are:

1. Trending strongly.
2. Outperforming Bitcoin.

Such assets should represent the strongest opportunities within the cryptocurrency universe.

---

## Expected Behaviour

Strong assets should rank highly under both factors.

Weak assets may score well under only one factor.

The combined score should improve asset selection quality.

---

## Success Criteria

- Sharpe Ratio greater than S07 (1.561)
- Improved equity growth
- Similar or lower drawdown
- Demonstrate independence between BTC Relative Strength and Momentum
