# S02 – Regime-Aware Momentum

## Motivation

The exploratory analysis revealed a strong asymmetry between bull and bear market environments.

Average Daily Return:

* Bull Market: +0.503%
* Bear Market: -0.199%

Additionally, cryptocurrency correlations increased during bear markets, reducing the effectiveness of diversification.

These findings suggest that remaining invested during unfavorable market conditions may be a major source of drawdown.

---

## Research Question

Can momentum performance be improved by conditioning portfolio exposure on the broader cryptocurrency market regime?

---

## Hypothesis

Momentum signals should perform best during bullish market environments where trends persist.

By restricting exposure to periods when Bitcoin is trading above its long-term trend, the strategy should:

* Improve risk-adjusted returns
* Reduce drawdowns
* Avoid prolonged bear market losses

---

## Expected Behaviour

### Bull Markets

Portfolio remains fully invested and captures momentum trends.

### Bear Markets

Portfolio moves to cash and avoids broad market declines.

---

## Success Criteria

Compared to S01:

* Higher Sharpe Ratio
* Lower Maximum Drawdown
* Similar Turnover
* Similar Holding Period
