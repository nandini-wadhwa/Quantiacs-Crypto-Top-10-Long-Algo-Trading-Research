# S08 – Correlation Regime

## Hypothesis

Periods of elevated cross-sectional correlation indicate systemic market stress.

When correlations become unusually high, diversification benefits disappear and portfolio risk increases.

Reducing exposure during high-correlation regimes should improve risk-adjusted performance.

## Expected Behaviour

Low Correlation:
- Diversification effective
- Full exposure

High Correlation:
- Diversification ineffective
- Reduce concentration
