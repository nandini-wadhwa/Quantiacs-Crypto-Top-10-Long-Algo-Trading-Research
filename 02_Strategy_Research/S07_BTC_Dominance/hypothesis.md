# S07 – BTC Dominance Strategy

## Motivation

Exploratory analysis revealed strong correlations between Bitcoin and the broader cryptocurrency universe.

Average pairwise correlation:

0.498

Many assets exhibited correlations above 0.70 with Bitcoin.

This suggests Bitcoin acts as the dominant market factor.

---

## Research Question

Can Bitcoin leadership be used as a predictive signal for cryptocurrency portfolio construction?

---

## Hypothesis

Assets that outperform Bitcoin during strong Bitcoin trends may represent the strongest opportunities in the market.

Bitcoin should be treated as the primary market driver rather than just another asset.

---

## Expected Behaviour

When Bitcoin trends upward:

- Strong altcoins should outperform.

When Bitcoin weakens:

- Exposure should be reduced.

---

## Success Criteria

- Sharpe Ratio above 1.0
- Better drawdown control
- Demonstrate usefulness of BTC as a factor
