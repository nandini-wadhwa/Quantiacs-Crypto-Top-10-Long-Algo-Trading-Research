# S06 – Volatility Compression

## Motivation

Exploratory analysis identified a positive relationship between volatility compression and future return magnitude.

Volatility Compression Correlation:

+0.0242

This suggests that assets experiencing unusually low short-term volatility may be preparing for larger future price movements.

---

## Research Question

Can volatility compression be used to identify future outperforming cryptocurrencies?

---

## Hypothesis

Periods of unusually low volatility are often followed by volatility expansion.

Assets exhibiting strong volatility compression should outperform the broader universe over subsequent periods.

---

## Expected Behaviour

Low-volatility consolidations often occur before trend continuation or breakout events.

The strategy attempts to identify these periods before expansion occurs.

---

## Success Criteria

- Sharpe Ratio greater than 1.0
- Positive equity growth
- Moderate turnover
- Strong diversification benefits
