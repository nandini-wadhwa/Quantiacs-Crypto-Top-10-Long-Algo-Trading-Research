# S01 – Cross-Sectional Momentum

## Motivation

Exploratory analysis identified positive momentum persistence within the cryptocurrency universe.

Momentum Persistence Correlation:

+0.0212

This suggests that assets exhibiting strong recent performance may continue outperforming over intermediate horizons.

---

## Research Question

Can a momentum-based ranking system generate positive risk-adjusted returns within the Quantiacs Crypto Top10 universe?

---

## Hypothesis

Assets with stronger recent returns will outperform weaker assets over subsequent periods.

A portfolio constructed from the strongest momentum assets should generate positive excess returns.

---

## Expected Behaviour

Bull Markets:
Momentum should perform well as trends persist.

Bear Markets:
Momentum may experience larger drawdowns due to market-wide declines.

---

## Success Criteria

- Positive long-term equity growth
- Sharpe Ratio > 1.0
- Consistent participation in major market trends
