# S05 – Volume Flow

## Motivation

Exploratory analysis identified a positive relationship between abnormal trading volume and future returns.

Volume Leadership Correlation:

+0.0481

This was the strongest predictive factor identified during the research phase.

The finding suggests that assets experiencing unusual trading activity may attract continued investor attention and subsequent price appreciation.

---

## Research Question

Can abnormal trading volume be used to identify future outperforming cryptocurrencies?

---

## Hypothesis

Assets exhibiting volume significantly above their recent average will outperform assets with normal trading activity.

A portfolio that systematically selects assets with abnormal volume should generate positive risk-adjusted returns.

---

## Expected Behaviour

### Bull Markets

Volume spikes should indicate capital inflows and trend continuation.

### Bear Markets

Volume spikes may indicate panic selling and weaker signal quality.

---

## Success Criteria

- Sharpe Ratio greater than S01
- Positive long-term equity growth
- Moderate turnover
- Consistent performance across market environments
