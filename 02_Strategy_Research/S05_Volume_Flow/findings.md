# Findings

## Research Outcome

The volume-based strategy generated positive risk-adjusted returns and achieved a Sharpe ratio above 1.

This validates the exploratory finding that abnormal trading volume contains predictive information regarding future cryptocurrency performance.

---

## What Worked

### Volume Contains Information

Assets experiencing abnormal trading volume tended to outperform the broader universe.

The strategy successfully monetized the volume leadership effect identified during exploratory analysis.

---

### Positive Risk-Adjusted Returns

The strategy achieved a Sharpe ratio of approximately 1.00 despite relying on a single factor.

This confirms that trading activity contains meaningful information beyond price alone.

---

## What Did Not Work

### Performance Remained Below Momentum

Despite having the strongest factor IC during exploratory analysis, the volume strategy underperformed the momentum strategy.

This suggests that volume alone does not fully capture the direction of future returns.

---

### Drawdowns Remained Significant

The strategy remained vulnerable to broad market downturns.

Volume spikes often occurred during both bullish and bearish environments.

---

## Key Insight

Volume appears to identify investor attention rather than directional conviction.

The factor is informative but likely requires additional directional information to reach its full potential.

---

## Research Conclusion

Volume is a viable source of alpha within cryptocurrency markets.

However, it appears most valuable when used as a confirmation signal rather than as a standalone trading factor.
