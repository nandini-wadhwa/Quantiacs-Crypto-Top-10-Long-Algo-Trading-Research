# S03 – Mean Reversion

## Motivation

Exploratory analysis identified a negative relationship between short-term returns and future returns.

Mean Reversion Correlation:

-0.0236

This suggests that cryptocurrencies experiencing recent underperformance may subsequently outperform.

---

## Research Question

Can a cross-sectional mean reversion strategy generate attractive risk-adjusted returns within the cryptocurrency universe?

---

## Hypothesis

Assets that have recently declined will outperform recent winners over short horizons.

A portfolio that systematically buys recent losers should generate positive returns.

---

## Expected Behaviour

### Range-Bound Markets

Mean reversion should perform well as prices oscillate around fair value.

### Trending Markets

Mean reversion may struggle as strong trends continue beyond the reversion horizon.

---

## Success Criteria

The strategy should:

* Generate positive risk-adjusted returns
* Exhibit behaviour distinct from momentum
* Provide diversification benefits relative to trend-following strategies
